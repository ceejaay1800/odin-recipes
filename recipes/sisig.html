Sisig

Description:

Sisig is probably the most famous Kapampangan dish ever! Get this easy and authentic Sisig recipe using grilled pork belly! Crunchy and spicy just the way it should be.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Pork Belly
  • 3 Pieces of Chicken Liver
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 Red Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Chili Peppors (minced)
  • 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps:

  • Step 1:Season pork belly slices with salt and pepper. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
